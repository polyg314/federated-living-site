Who we are

Federated Living Solutions is dedicated to making property ownership and investing accessible through creative financing. We believe that flexible, well-structured deals benefit both property owners and buyers, and we work to find solutions that fit your goals.

About creative financing

Creative financing goes beyond conventional bank loans. It includes seller financing, where the seller acts as the lender; lease options that let you control and eventually own property; and subject-to deals that assume existing mortgages. These strategies can unlock opportunities when traditional financing isn't available or desirable. We help you understand your options and structure agreements that are clear, compliant, and aligned with your objectives.
