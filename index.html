Federated Living Solutions

Who We Are

Federated Living Solutions is a real estate investment company focused on making property ownership and investing more accessible through creative financing and strategic deal structuring.

We believe real estate opportunities should not be limited to those who qualify for traditional bank lending. By using flexible financing strategies and innovative acquisition methods, we help create pathways for buyers, sellers, and investors to achieve their goals.

Our mission is simple: to structure real estate transactions that create opportunity beyond the limitations of conventional financing.

What We Do

Federated Living Solutions specializes in acquiring and structuring residential real estate transactions through creative financing.

Our work includes solutions such as seller financing, mortgage-takeover acquisitions, and other flexible transaction structures that allow properties to move forward when traditional financing may not be the best option.

By focusing on collaboration and thoughtful deal design, we aim to structure transactions that create value for all parties involved — from property owners looking for alternatives to traditional sales, to investors seeking opportunities in the market.

Our Approach

Every property and every situation is different. Our approach is centered around understanding the goals of everyone involved and designing a structure that aligns with those objectives.

We focus on:

  • Transparent communication
  • Flexible deal structuring
  • Responsible long-term investing
  • Creating mutually beneficial outcomes whenever possible

Rather than forcing a single model onto every opportunity, we evaluate each deal individually and look for solutions that make practical and financial sense.

Education & Coaching

In addition to investing, Federated Living Solutions provides one-on-one coaching for individuals interested in learning real estate investing and creative finance strategies.

Through these sessions we share our experience, frameworks, and thought process behind analyzing deals, structuring acquisitions, and navigating real-world investment scenarios.

Our goal is to help others develop the knowledge and confidence needed to approach real estate investing with clarity and discipline.

Our Vision

We believe real estate has the power to create long-term financial opportunity and stability when approached with the right knowledge and strategy.

Federated Living Solutions exists to expand access to those opportunities by connecting motivated buyers, sellers, and investors through creative solutions that make property transactions possible.
